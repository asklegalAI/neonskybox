The Story

Welcome to Neon Muertos, where the vibrant traditions of Día de los Muertos merge with a neon-infused cyberpunk future. In this electrifying version of New York City in 2099, the boundary between the living and the dead has beautifully blurred, creating a year-round celebration of life, death, and everything in between.

Our tale follows Luna and Zephyr, star-crossed lovers entangled in a web of corporate intrigue and ancestral magic. As leaders of the Neon Skulls, a gang of high-tech rebels, they navigate the treacherous landscape of both physical and digital worlds, uncovering a conspiracy that threatens the delicate balance between the realms of the living and the dead.
